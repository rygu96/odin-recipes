Roasted Chicken Recipe

Home

Description:

A recipe for that involves roasting chicken, resulting in juicy and tender chicken. Dish can be served with additional sides.

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken
  • 1 tablespoon of onion powder
  • 1/2 cup margarine
  • 1 stalk celery

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Place chicken in a large pan, and add seasoning
  3. Bake chicken for about 1 hour and 15 minutes. Remove from heat and let rest for 30 minutes.
