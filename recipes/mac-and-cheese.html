Mac and Cheese Recipe

Home

Description:

A simple dish consisting of macaroni and cheese as the primary ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • 8oz of macaroni
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup of flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • Ground pepper
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 cups of cheddar cheese

Steps:

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil, then cook macaroni until it is tender
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan, add listed seasonings and stir for about 5 minutes
  3. Add cheddar cheese and stir until it is melted
  4. Drain macaroni and add to cheese sauce
