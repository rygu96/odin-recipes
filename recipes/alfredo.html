Description:

Chicken alfredo is a classic favoriteâ€”a creamy white sauce, long strands of pasta mixed with tender pieces of lean protein.

Ingredients:

  • Fettuccine
  • Chicken breast
  • 1 stick of butter
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • freshly grated nutmeg
  • Noodles of choice

Steps:

  1. Season chicken and cook until no pink is showing
  2. Bring water to a boil and add noodles. Cook until noodles are desired firmness
  3. Add alfredo sauce to chicken and season with additional spices
  4. Drain cooked pasta and serve with chicken and alfredo sauce
